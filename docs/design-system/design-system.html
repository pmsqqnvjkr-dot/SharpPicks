App icon

The app icon uses Sharp Navy #111827 as its background. The only place this color appears in the system. Tested against ~30 wallpaper photos to ensure it holds against both bright and dark home screens.

Two-spec system: the app icon uses LIGHT bar weight (3.8% × 34% of icon dimensions), intentionally different from the wordmark and standalone signal mark which use M2 medium. At notification-badge sizes (29×29px) and tab icon sizes (60×60px), bar widths are measured in fractional pixels. Anything heavier than 3.8% width turns into chunky rectangles that lose typographic identity. Do not "harmonize" the wordmark and app icon weights. They serve different rendering contexts.

Voice rules

Required posture
  • Bloomberg analyst, not ESPN personality
  • Specific over general
  • Falsifiable over vague
  • Short over long
  • "10 of 14 edges on dogs" not "lots of underdog action"
Forbidden in all copy
  • No em-dashes (use periods)
  • No exclamation marks (zero exceptions)
  • No emoji anywhere
  • No hyphens-as-sentence-separators
  • No AI-speak ("let's dive in")
  • No CAPS for emphasis
  • No gambling slang ("lock", "hammer", "smash")
  • No hype words ("incredible", "huge", "fire")

Empty states & pass days

A pass day is a day where no signal cleared the threshold. SharpPicks treats this as proof the system is working, not as a failure.

CAPITAL PRESERVED
No signals cleared the threshold today.
The slate did not produce edges meeting the discipline filter. Capital preserved for tomorrow.
Pass day rules
  • Eyebrow always reads "Capital preserved"
  • Reference Sharp Principle within 1-2 sentences
  • Use neutral blue, never red
  • Single combined card, not multiple
Forbidden on pass days
  • No red anywhere
  • No "L" badges on withdrawn picks
  • No "Pass day" or "No signal" as primary framing
  • No triple-negative card stacks (the old pattern)

Error states

Errors in SharpPicks are rare and handled with restraint. No fire-truck red, no modal panic, no exclamation marks.

Sync failed. Pull to refresh.

Motion & animation

Motion in SharpPicks is restrained. The institutional voice extends to interaction.

Locked motion
  • Pulse on amber dots (2s cycle)
  • Fade in (200ms ease-out)
  • Slide up (240ms ease-out, 8px)
Forbidden motion
  • No bounce or spring physics
  • No parallax scrolling
  • No animated number counters
  • No hover lifts (translate/scale)
  • No confetti or celebration animations
