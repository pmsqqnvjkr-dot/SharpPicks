Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: Email address and password (hashed) when you create an account.

Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, and device information to improve the product.

Payment Information: Processed by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers.

Push Notification Tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens for delivering notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the SharpPicks service
  • Send push notifications for picks, results, and alerts (with your consent)
  • Process subscription payments via Stripe
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Respond to support requests

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe — Payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase (Google) — Push notifications and analytics (Firebase Privacy)
  • Railway — Application hosting

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the app's Account settings, which permanently removes all your data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is shared only with service providers listed above, solely to operate the service.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time

California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what data we collect, request deletion, and opt out of data sales (we do not sell data).

EU/EEA residents (GDPR): You may exercise your rights under GDPR by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

SharpPicks is intended for users 21 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 21.

8. Security

We use HTTPS encryption, hashed passwords, and secure session management to protect your data. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
