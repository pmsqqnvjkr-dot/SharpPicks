CLV+ at 0%
Closing Line Value of 0% means your picks aren’t beating the closing line on average. This is the sharpest single metric of edge quality — beating CLV consistently is what separates real edges from variance. 11 picks is too few to conclude anything, but this is the number to watch most carefully as you scale. If CLV+ stays near zero at 50 picks, the model isn’t finding real edges before the market closes them.
