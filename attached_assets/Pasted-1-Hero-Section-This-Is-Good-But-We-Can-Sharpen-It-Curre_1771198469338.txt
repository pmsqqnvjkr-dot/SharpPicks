1️⃣ Hero Section – This Is Good, But We Can Sharpen It

Current:

Full access to every qualified pick.
The model runs daily — Pro sees the complete decision.
This is not more picks. It’s full transparency.
This is how disciplined bettors compound.

That’s strong.

I would tighten to:

Sharp Picks Pro

Full access to every qualified decision.
Not more picks. More transparency.
This is how disciplined bettors compound.

Cleaner. Fewer words. More authority.

Then your subtle box:

When your trial ends, the full decision disappears.

🔥 That’s good psychology.
It frames loss without being aggressive.

3️⃣ Founding Scarcity Banner – This Needs Precision

Right now:

2 of 500 claimed
498 left

That feels… early. Which reduces urgency.

Instead say:

Founding Members: 498 of 500 remaining

Cleaner. Neutral. No weird math.

Or even sharper:

Founding Rate closes at 500 members.

498 remaining.

Let the scarcity feel structural, not promotional.
