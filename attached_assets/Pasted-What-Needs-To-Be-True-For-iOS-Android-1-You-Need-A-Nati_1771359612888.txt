What Needs To Be True For iOS + Android

1. You Need A Native Shell (Wrapper)

Your site must be wrapped in:
	•	React Native
	•	OR Flutter
	•	OR simplest: Capacitor (Ionic)

For you?

👉 Capacitor wrapper around your existing app
Fastest path. Keeps everything web-based.

It turns:
https://app.sharppicks.ai
into a real .ipa (iOS) and .aab (Android) file.

2. Apple Requirements

To ship on iOS:

You must have:
	•	Apple Developer Account ($99/year)
	•	Privacy Policy page
	•	Terms of Service
	•	Proper push notification setup (APNs via Firebase)
	•	No gambling language in store listing

Critical:

Apple is very sensitive to:
	•	Sports betting
	•	Gambling monetization
	•	Performance claims

You are fine if:
	•	You position as sports analytics
	•	No sportsbook links
	•	No “bet now”
	•	No affiliate tracking
	•	Clear disclaimer

Your positioning is already smart:
“Model picks”
“Selective by design”
“Process over hype”

Keep it that way.

⸻

3. Google Play Requirements

Google is slightly more forgiving.

You’ll need:
	•	Google Play Developer account ($25 one-time)
	•	Content rating form
	•	Data safety form
	•	Privacy policy
	•	Proper push setup (Firebase already good)

Android is easier approval-wise.

⸻

4. Compliance Layer (Very Important)

You must add:

In-app disclaimer page:

SharpPicks provides sports analytics and model-based insights. Not financial advice. Not a sportsbook.

No emojis.
No hype.
No “guaranteed.”

You’re already positioned correctly.

⸻

5. Technical Checklist

Before submission:

Must be true:
	•	HTTPS everywhere
	•	No console errors
	•	No broken routes
	•	Push works on:
	•	iOS (native)
	•	Android
	•	App icon sizes correct
	•	Splash screen
	•	Offline fallback page
	•	Version number system

You already have:
