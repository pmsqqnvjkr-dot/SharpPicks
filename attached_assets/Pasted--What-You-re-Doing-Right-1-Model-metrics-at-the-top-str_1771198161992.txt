1️⃣ The “498 / 500” Issue

Right now you show:

498

This reads confusing.

It looks like 498 remaining out of 500… which implies only 2 sold.

You want it to feel like it’s almost gone.

Fix it like this:

Option A (clean + premium):

498 of 500 claimed

Option B (strong scarcity):

Only 2 founding spots left

Don’t show the raw 498 number floating alone on the right. It feels mechanical.
