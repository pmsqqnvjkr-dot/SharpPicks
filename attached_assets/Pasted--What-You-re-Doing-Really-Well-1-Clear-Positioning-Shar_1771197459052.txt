🚨 Where It Can Convert Even Harder

1️⃣ The Founding Scarcity Needs Teeth

You show:

498 / 500

That’s great. But psychologically?

You want them to feel:
	•	“I’m about to miss this”
	•	Not “Oh cool, almost full”

Upgrade suggestion:

Instead of just:

Founding member spots – 498/500

Add micro-copy like:

Only 2 founding spots remain at $99/year.

Even if it dynamically updates. That urgency is what tips fence-sitters.

⸻

2️⃣ Your CTA Hierarchy Needs One Clear Winner

Right now:
	•	Subscribe Monthly
	•	Claim Founding Rate

Visually they’re similar weight.

You want the Founding rate to feel like the smart move.

Consider:
	•	Highlight Founding in subtle gold glow
	•	Add small “Best Value” tag
	•	Or reorder so Founding appears first

Psychology rule:
People choose the option that looks “intended.”

Right now they’re choosing.
You want to guide.
